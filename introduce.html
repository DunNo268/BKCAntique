Company Profile

Our company was established in 2002, with 20 years of experience in the field of selling antiques, we are proud to be the largest and most prestigious company, receiving a lot of trust from big guests from all over the world.

Our first and main headquarters are located in the hometown of the president - founder of the company. It is at Building A17, Floor 02, No. 17 Ta Quang Buu, Bach Khoa, Hai Ba Trung, Hanoi.

BKC Antiques and Auctions brings together 638 antique dealers and customers into a single online marketplace. Using BKC Web

If you are interested to buy an antique that you have seen on BKC, please contact the antique dealer directly using the contact details shown with the item.

All communications occur between the buyer and the antique dealer, BKC is not involved or responsible for terms of sale.

Exchange rates are updated daily.

When an antique dealer uploads an item for display on BKC they are requested to make a formal declaration as to the date of its manufacture. This date is shown on each antique detail page. The use of the word Circa or C. is to indicate that the date is "close to" date shown.
Eg. Circa 1920 means close to the year 1920.
